Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, UPI payments, and online payment gateways like Razorpay and Paytm.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll only pay the prorated difference for the remaining billing period. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans with no credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period.

What happens if I exceed my product limit?

If you approach your product limit, we'll notify you in advance. You can either archive unused products or upgrade your plan to accommodate more products.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for registered non-profit organizations and charitable pharmacies. Please contact our sales team with your registration details to learn more.

How is data backed up?

All data is automatically backed up daily with multiple redundant copies stored in geographically separate locations. We maintain a 30-day backup history that you can restore from at any time.
