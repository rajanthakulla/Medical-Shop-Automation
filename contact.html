Common Questions

What are your support hours?

Our standard support hours are Monday through Friday, 9AM to 6PM IST. For enterprise customers, we offer 24/7 priority support.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 1 business day. Urgent support requests are prioritized and usually receive a response within 4 hours.

Do you offer on-site training?

Yes, we provide on-site training for enterprise customers. For smaller pharmacies, we offer comprehensive online training sessions and documentation.

Can I schedule a product demo?

Absolutely! You can schedule a personalized demo with our sales team through our demo page or by contacting us directly.
